How is this calculated?

  • Alias Normalization: Automatically maps framework variations to a canonical root (e.g., mapping "React.js" or "ReactJS" simply to "React").
  • Abbreviation Handling: Successfully recognizes industry shorthand, linking terms like "K8s" to "Kubernetes" and "AWS Cloud" to "AWS".
  • Fuzzy Fallback: Uses a 90% similarity cutoff via difflib to gracefully catch slight typos or misspellings in candidate resumes before marking a skill as missing.
  • Gap Analysis: Cross-references normalized lists to explicitly isolate and report which required JD skills are missing from the profile.
