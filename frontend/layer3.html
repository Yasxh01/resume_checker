How is this calculated?

  • NLP Timeline Extraction (60%): Automatically parses complex date formats (e.g., "Jan 2019 - Present") and forcefully merges overlapping intervals to prevent double-counting concurrent roles.
  • Seniority Classification (40%): Scans resume text for linguistic signals (e.g., "Lead", "Architect", "Mentored") to classify the candidate as Junior, Mid, Senior, Staff, or Exec independently of stated years.
  • Anti-Gaming Logic: By cross-validating the objective parsed timeline with contextual seniority markers, the composite score aggressively resists title inflation and manual input padding.
